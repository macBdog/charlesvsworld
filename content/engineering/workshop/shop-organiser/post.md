Picked up some really cheap parts storage bins a few weeks ago then knocked this organiser up from scrap:

![](http://charles.henden.com.au/sites/default/files/styles/media_gallery_large/public/IMG_20160523_170259.jpg?itok=Y3gDCp5T)

![](http://charles.henden.com.au/sites/default/files/styles/media_gallery_large/public/IMG_20160518_162843.jpg?itok=oK5sgrCt)
Started out with some 1mm zinc sheet and 50x25mm tube scrap.

![](http://charles.henden.com.au/sites/default/files/styles/media_gallery_large/public/IMG_20160518_164208.jpg?itok=se4u5qBV)
Turned mounted the sheet and made a stand.

![](http://charles.henden.com.au/sites/default/files/styles/media_gallery_large/public/IMG_20160518_215704.jpg?itok=XZA2uoUI)
Some wheels

![](http://charles.henden.com.au/sites/default/files/styles/media_gallery_large/public/IMG_20160522_172236.jpg?itok=wjPMKXW8)
Cut out some brackets with the plasma cutter to hold steel rods for tapes and rolls of materials

![](http://charles.henden.com.au/sites/default/files/styles/media_gallery_large/public/IMG_20160523_160555.jpg?itok=j7RrHBIL)
Quick lick of paint then loaded up
