It may come as a great surprise to see the 924 race car project move into the twilight of all project cars: doing what it was intended. 

![](MR513810.jpg)

That's right, it's now a functional racecar shaving down seconds of laptime at QR, Lakeside and Morgan park.

[Between dyno runs](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k6wKQ1EJKck) and regular track days, the level of satisfaction in finishing this project is amoungst the tippy-top of all ventures I've attempted. Just in time to be mothballed in anticipation for another move across the pacific ocean ;)
