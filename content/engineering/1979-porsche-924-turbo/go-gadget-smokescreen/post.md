F#cking hell I have converted my car into a crop duster.

Saturday arvo I took it out for the first drive in a few months. Coming out of the driveway I heard the new turbo spool up... it wasnt pretty

It had a medium pitch very loud whine that sounded like metal on metal rubbing action. There was some oil smoke when I was driving down the road. I turned on to Milton road and planted my foot to get up the hill next to the brewery and released a smoke cloud of epic proportions. People were turning on their highbeams to drive through the oil-smoke cloud that was quickly encompassing the whole suburb, man was it embarrasing.

Time to ring up option1garage and get angry. This supposedly brand new turbo was whining like a b!tch, dumping the full payload of oil straight into the exhaust and making absolutly no boost.
