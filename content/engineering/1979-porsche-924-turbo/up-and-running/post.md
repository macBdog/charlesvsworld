Woohoo! Idled and ran smoothly @ 2am last night.

 

![](ready1.jpg)

Took a fair bit of cranking to realise a few things

* Coil pack needs 12v not GND despite the wiring diagram

	* Firing order is 1342 so coil pinout is 1423 in a wasted spark setup

	* Fuel pump relay or wiring is buggered (had to jumper the relay)

So after spark, air and fuel were happening correctly the car fired right up, idled smooth and revved freely. After a minute or so of running there was smoke coming from around the exhaust manifold but I expected this because I used so much degreaser and paint and gasket stuff all over the hot side of the engine.

Cant wait to have a drive!
