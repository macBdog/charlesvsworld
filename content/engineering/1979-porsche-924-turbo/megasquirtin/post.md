I finished building the [MegaSquirt ECU](http://megasquirt.info) last night. I went with a V2.2 board for MS1. The other box its connected to is the MS relay board which is a great convenience. I chose to use a relay board instead of the stimulator to test and that worked fine. The assembly was very well organized and the board tested fine at the end so I can't wait to get it in the car and turn the key.
