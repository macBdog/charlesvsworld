Progress on the 911 has been slow but constant. The biggest piece of work this weekend was mounting the ECUs up in the passenger compartment.

![](ecus.jpg)

Here is a picture of the engine bay stripped of its rats-nest wiring loom. Note the 3.0L intake installed for its TPS compatable throttle body.

![](engine1.jpg)

The aforementioned rats nest:

![](messy_loom.jpg)

To date we have: 

*Removed the ECUs from their resting place in the engine compartment (heat and vibration sensitive components...) and re-mounted them at the front of the car. 

*Stripped the wiring loom from the engine bay 

*Removed all offensive pieces of the interior, replaced the supercheap racing seats with Porsche OE seats and modified the rails to suit. 

*Brakes have been rebuilt, discs turned and new brake lines made up.
